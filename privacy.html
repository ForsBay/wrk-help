Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Work Hours app collects and how it is used.

Local storage

The app stores configuration, tracked hours, calendar event IDs, and overtime info locally in your browser via localStorage.

Google authentication

If you choose to sign in, the app uses Firebase Authentication with Google to identify your account. No personal data is stored in the app beyond your display name, email, and photo URL needed for the profile UI.

Calendar access

Calendar access is requested only when you enable calendar sync. If enabled, the app may create or update events in your primary Google Calendar. This is optional and only used for event syncing.

Third-party services

The app uses Firebase and Google APIs. Your data is transmitted to these services only for authentication and optional sync.

Data deletion

You can delete entries in the app at any time. To delete remote data, sign out and remove your account data from the Firebase console if needed.

If you do not accept this policy, please do not use the app or enable Google sync.
